Instructions

To use Smart Attendance System, register with valid credentials and upload a clear passport size photo (without spectacles)

While marking attendance look directly in front of the webcam, ensure proper lighting

Students are advised to put off their spectacles for better accuracy while marking attendance .

Entry after 10 minutes of class commencement is marked as 'Late Attendance'. So try to arrive as early as possible .
